About

Thomas K. Moore is a drawing and animation artist with a passion for character design, creature concepts, and visual storytelling. Rooted in traditional drawing fundamentals, his work spans detailed figure studies, imaginative creature designs, and hand-drawn animation.

With a sketchbook always within reach, Thomas explores anatomy, armor, and narrative through line and form - blending observational study with inventive world-building. His animation work brings these characters and ideas to life with expressive motion and careful timing.
